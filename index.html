Setup Guide
1
Buy any Bluetooth AB Shutter 3 remote clicker (~R80–150 online or at phone accessory shops). Works on Android and iPhone.
2
Put your phone's Bluetooth on. Press and hold the button on the clicker until it flashes — it enters pairing mode.
3
Pair the clicker in your phone's Bluetooth settings like any device. Name is usually "AB Shutter3" or "Camera Shutter".
4
Once paired, the clicker sends a volume key press when clicked. Tap Connect above and press the button — Flow Four will detect it and use it as your tap input.
5
Clip or velcro the button to your wristband or net post. Press once per shot — the rally counter goes up automatically.

Scoreboard Setup — 3 Options
A
Phone/tablet on a stand — Open this app on a second device and go to the scoreboard URL shown above. It auto-updates in real time over your hotspot or WiFi. Mount on a tripod or stake next to the court.
B
Outdoor LED display — Any LED panel with a browser (Android TV stick or Raspberry Pi plugged in) can show the scoreboard page full-screen. Recommended: Xiaomi TV Stick + outdoor screen, ~R800–1,500 total.
C
Chromecast / Apple TV — Cast the scoreboard page from Chrome or Safari to any screen near the court. Works over a mobile hotspot if no WiFi is available.
